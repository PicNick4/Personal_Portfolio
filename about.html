About Me

I am a 21 year old from Charlotte, North Carolina. I have been a fan of technology every since I was young and was always into things like computers, phones, and video games. I went to DePaul University in Chicago for computer science but ended up leaving after 2 years to go back home and work to save money. I have still been passionate about coding and working with technology and still find it very interesting and fun. I also am a big sports fan and have liked all the Chicago teams such as the Bears, Bulls, and Cubs for my entire life. I was originally born in Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania but moved to Charlotte when I was 4 years old and have lived here ever since.
